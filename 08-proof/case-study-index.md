## Rules for Using Proof

1. Only reference confirmed client work — do not invent outcomes.
2. Do not publish client names or details without confirmation of approval.
3. Do not exaggerate outcomes (no "saved X hours" or "recovered $Y" without verified data).
4. Published case studies can be referenced freely.
5. Unpublished work can be referenced vaguely ("we have helped stores with...") but not by name without approval.
